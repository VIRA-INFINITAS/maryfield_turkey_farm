Meet Our Turkeys

We have reared turkeys at Maryfield since 1981.

Our turkeys roam freely in the beautiful Deeside countryside, enjoying the fresh Scottish air and lush green pastures of Maryfield Farm, near Banchory.

The Breed

Bronze Turkey

At Maryfield Farm, we specialize in Bronze Turkeys, a premium breed known for:

Superior Flavour & Texture

These Turkeys have a higher meat-to-bone ratio, producing tender and flavourful meat.

Slow Natural Growth

Unlike fast-growing commercial breeds, they develop at a natural pace, enhancing texture and depth of flavour.

Instinctive Foraging

They naturally forage for insects, grass, and wild plants, contributing to their well-balanced diet and unique taste.

We Are Proud to Be a Golden Turkey Accredited Farm

Holding the Golden Turkey Quality Assurance certification means a commitment to exceptional farming standards and uncompromising quality.

This accreditation is awarded only to farms that follow traditional, high-welfare practices, ensuring the best possible turkey for your table.

Why Choose a Golden Turkey?

100% Free-Range – Raised outdoors with plenty of space to roam

Slow-Grown to Full Maturity – Twice the age of commercially farmed turkeys

Quality Diet – Fed on a natural, cereal-based diet

Hand-Finished & Dry Plucked – Ensuring perfect presentation and premium quality

Dry-Aged for Superior Flavour – Deep, rich taste with a succulent texture

Only from Accredited UK Family Farms – Guaranteeing provenance and ethical farming
